About Us

According to the user research we conducted, the majority of international students feel a severe sense of isolation and loneliness because they tend to not have an intimate community in the U.S. Unfortunately, international students are observed to be more reluctant to seek help due to the lack of cultural familiarity and different emotional norms that support the mental problem (Pupavac, 2004). Therefore, international students are relatively more prone to risks of depression and other mental health problems. We aim to support international students by providing a platform that could aid in improving their mental health.
